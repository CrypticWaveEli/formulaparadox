Take a rest!

Squinting, you look up to the position of the sun in the sky; Looks to be around lunch time. A sudden angry grumble from your stomach confirms your suspicions. There is a nice big shady palm tree just ahead, it would be a good place to rest for a moment, drink some water and inspect the contents of your rucksack.

You head for the tree and unceremoniously drop your bag from your aching shoulders and and flop down to the ground in a sweaty exhausted heap. Dumping out out the contents of your bag on the sandy ground in front of you, you find a deadly sharp machete, a ferro rod, a small metal pot to cook and boil water, a smaller but no less sharp hunting knife, a good sized metal water bottle, manila file stamped “CONFIDENTIAL” in big red official looking letters, a map and a compass.

Picking up the metal bottle, you drink deeply but not too deeply, the water is sweet and cool and intensely refreshing but you stop yourself short; you haven't found a source of fresh water yet.

The water and shade have helped a little but Im not used to this kind of heat and exhertion! Maybe a quick nap?
